Kaiser Workforce Planning Analytics

Sanitized Case Study | Capacity Modeling | Forecasting | Staffing Strategy

Confidential client project — this public version uses mock data, generalized team names, and recreated visuals to demonstrate the methodology while protecting sensitive information.

Business Problem

Leadership needed a clearer way to evaluate workload distribution, capacity pressure, turnaround time, staffing gaps, and future workforce needs during an operating model transition.

Methodology

1. Data Cleaning

Filtered completed intake records, corrected missing or out-of-order timestamps, and standardized lifecycle stages.

2. TAT Feature Engineering

Created assignment, pre-work, execution, closure, and total turnaround time metrics.

3. Capacity Analysis

Compared forecasted demand against estimated monthly capacity to identify utilization pressure and staffing gaps.

4. Core-Flex Recommendation

Mapped teams into stable core, flexible delivery, and floating pool recommendations based on demand patterns.
